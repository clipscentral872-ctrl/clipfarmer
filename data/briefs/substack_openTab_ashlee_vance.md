How to clip
Open Tab is a long-form interview show. Your job is to pull the punchiest, most-shareable moments out of a longer conversation.

Good clip ideas:
- A bold or counterintuitive take from Ashlee on tech, founders, Silicon Valley, building, or media.
- A laugh-out-loud / quotable moment between Ashlee and the hosts.
- A story he tells with a strong narrative arc — setup, twist, payoff.
- Any moment where what he says would make people want to send it to a friend.

Edit guidelines:
- Quick cuts — no dead time at the start.
- Big captions (white text, black outline) so people can watch on mute.
- Hook in the first 2 seconds — start on Ashlee's strongest line.
- Vertical 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts. 16:9 fine for X.
