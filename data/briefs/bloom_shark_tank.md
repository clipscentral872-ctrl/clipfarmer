Content Guidelines

What Makes a Good Clip
✓ Best moments from the Shark Tank pitch
✓ Strong hook in the first 1-2 seconds
✓ 15-60 seconds long
✓ Captions and basic editing (SFX, zoom-ins)
✓ Feels native to the platform
✓ Highlights the story: two college students, built in a dorm, pitched on Shark Tank
✓ Shows the product in a positive, engaging light

Content Dos
✓ Clip from the Shark Tank episode (primary focus)
✓ Add captions on every clip
✓ Tag @tap.bloom and include #bloomcard
✓ Lean into the college students / dorm room founder story
✓ Use trending audios when they fit

Content Don'ts
✗ No profanity
✗ No competitor mentions (do NOT mention other screen time or focus apps)
✗ No AI-generated content
✗ No low quality or blurry clips
✗ No raw unedited footage with zero editing
✗ No content that makes the product or founders look bad
✗ Do NOT buy views, likes, or engagement. You will be banned.
✗ Do NOT use content from outside the approved sources
